_Max

Evaluates which of the two numbers is higher

#include <Math.au3>
_Max ( $iNum1, $iNum2 )

ParametersParamètres

$iNum1 First number.
$iNum2 Second number.

Return ValueValeur de retour

Success:Succès : the higher of the two numbers.
Failure:Échec : 0 and sets the @error flag to non-zero
@error:@error : 1 - $iNum1 isn't a number.
2 - $iNum2 isn't a number.

RemarksRemarques

This function works with floats as well as integers.
