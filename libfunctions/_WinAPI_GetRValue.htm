_WinAPI_GetRValue

Retrieves an intensity value for the red component of a 32-bit RGB value

#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>
_WinAPI_GetRValue ( $iRGB )

ParametersParamètres

$iRGB The color value, in RGB.

Return ValueValeur de retour

Returns the intensity of the red component of the specified RGB color.

ExampleExemple

#include <WinAPIGdi.au3>

Local Const $RGB = 0xFF8040

Local $R = _WinAPI_GetRValue($RGB)
Local $G = _WinAPI_GetGValue($RGB)
Local $B = _WinAPI_GetBValue($RGB)

ConsoleWrite('Red: 0x' & Hex($R, 2) & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('Green: 0x' & Hex($G, 2) & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('Blue: 0x' & Hex($B, 2) & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('---------------' & @CRLF)
ConsoleWrite('RGB: 0x' & Hex(_WinAPI_RGB($R, $G, $B), 6) & @CRLF)
