_Radian

Converts degrees to radians

#include <Math.au3>
_Radian ( $iDegrees )

ParametersParamètres

$iDegrees Degrees to be converted into radians.

Return ValueValeur de retour

Success:Succès : the radians converted from degrees.
Failure:Échec : an empty string and sets the @error flag to non-zero.Retourne une chaîne vide et @error <> 0.

RemarksRemarques

Can be used in conjunction with the Sin(), Cos(), and Tan() functions.

RelatedLiens

_Degree

ExampleExemple

#include <Math.au3>
#include <MsgBoxConstants.au3>

Local $fDegrees = 35
Local $fRadians = _Radian($fDegrees)

MsgBox($MB_SYSTEMMODAL, "Degrees to Radians", $fDegrees & " degrees = " & $fRadians & " radians")
